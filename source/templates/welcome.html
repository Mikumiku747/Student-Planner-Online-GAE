What is student planner online?

Student planner online is a website that is made to help students with thier school life in a simple and easy way, from a range of devices.

What can student planner online do?

When I made student planner online, it was because I was lazy. I didn't like using our school diaries, as they were bulky, ugly, and annoying to get out and write in every time I wanted to put an event down. So I made this website to replace it. This site currently replaces my school diary, but soon it will have a study tracker, with a study log, tracker, goals and more.

Right now, you can:

  • Store your timetable online, and view it on your phone, tablet or computer.
  • Create and manage events, helping you to keep track of important dates and times such as assignments or social outings.
  • Soon to come: Study log, with tracking and goals, to help you motivate yourself to study.

Online timetable

You'll never have to worry about forgetting your timetable again with the online timetable, that has space for teachers, class names, locations, and more. It formats to appear in the most convenient form, from a single day's overview to viewing the entire week, depending on your screen size.

Online timetable

Keep track of your school, social and working life with events. They provide you with a quick list of things coming up in a glance. No longer will you forget about that pesky assignment that you were procrastinating against, or that lunch classmates that you forgot to go to. Events keeps the happenings in your life under control.
